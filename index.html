FICTIONAL VILLIAN

NAME : JOKER


FIRST APPEARANCE : Batman Comics( APRIL 25,1940 )


AGE : 25 years


OVERVIEW :

The Joker is a supervillain appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by Bill Finger, Bob Kane, and Jerry Robinson, and first appeared in the debut issue of the comic book Batman on April 25, 1940.the Joker is portrayed as a criminal mastermind. Introduced as a psychopath with a warped, sadistic sense of humor.
